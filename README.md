# ELLA Sports Foundation Participation Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes community participation patterns in youth sports programs to identify barriers to engagement and opportunities for increased involvement.

The analysis was conducted during my research internship with ELLA Sports Foundation.

## Research Question
What factors influence participation in youth sports programs within underserved communities?

## Methods
- Needs assessment surveys
- Descriptive statistical analysis
- Participation trend analysis
- Stakeholder feedback

## Tools Used
SPSS
Excel
Python (if applicable)

## Key Insights
- Transportation barriers reduced participation rates
- Weekend programs had higher attendance
- Community outreach improved enrollment in certain programs

## Impact
Findings were presented to ELLA leadership to support program strategy improvements and community engagement initiatives.
